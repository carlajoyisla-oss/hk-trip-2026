🛺 Stroller & Family Tips

Everything you need for a smooth trip with two toddlers

🚇

MTR Elevators

Every station has lifts — look for wheelchair symbols on maps. Avoid rush hours 7:30–9:30AM & 5:30–7:30PM with the stroller.

👶

Baby Carrier

Bring one for Temple Street, Peak Tram stairs, and Ladies' Market where strollers get tricky.

🚌

FREE Hotel Shuttle

Panda Hotel runs free shuttles to Tsim Sha Tsui & Mong Kok. Check the schedule at front desk each morning!

💳

Octopus Card

Use for MTR, buses, 7-Eleven & restaurants. Buy at airport. Kids 3–11 need a child Octopus.

☀️

July Heat

~31°C, very humid. Duck into free AC malls for nap breaks: Panda Place, Harbour City (TST), Plaza Hollywood (Diamond Hill).

🌧️

Typhoon Season

Download HK Observatory app. If Signal No. 8 is raised, most attractions close — have an indoor backup!

🏔

The Peak Tram Pro Tip

Book online at discoverhongkong.com for slight discounts! Early morning (before 9AM) is best — fewer crowds.

😴

Protect the Naps!

Days 3 & 4: mandatory hotel naps at afternoon. Everyone (especially toddlers!) enjoys the evenings SO much more after rest!
